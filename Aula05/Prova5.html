This website is a nature photo gallery, created to highlight the beauty of the natural world through photography. The main focus is to present images of landscapes, natural environments, and also animals in their habitats, valuing every detail captured. The gallery brings together photos of forests, mountains, rivers, mares, and different animal species, providing a tranquil and inspiring visual experience. The goal is to allow the visitor to explore nature in a simple way, with clear and organized navigation. The website was developed to be intuitive and accessible, working well on different devices and facilitating the viewing of images.
